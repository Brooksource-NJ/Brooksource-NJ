## About Brooksource

Brooksource is a national Engineering and Technology solutions provider delivering talent, consulting, and workforce transformation services to organizations across North America.

For more than two decades, we have partnered with enterprise clients to solve complex business challenges through technology expertise, strategic consulting, and high-performing teams.

Our focus is simple: connecting exceptional people with meaningful opportunities while helping organizations accelerate innovation and achieve long-term success.
